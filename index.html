Dev Diwali Booking

Enjoy the Grandest festival in Varanasi

    The Boat ride will take you through the scintillating and nicely decorated Ghats and monuments. From a welcome on the boat, to relishing some local snacks, enjoy the Dev Diwali Festival with newer friends on the boat and immerse your senses in the energy of Banaras. Dev Diwali is a festival celebrated in the holy city of Varanasi 2 weeks after the real Diwali ``the festival of lights``. Dev Diwali is celebrated by Gods when they come down to the Ghats of Varanasi and celebrate the death of demon ``Tripurasura`` killed by Lord Shiva. On this day , every Ghat along the Ganges is lit with oil lamps and diyas. At different ghats, events and cultural programmes are being arranged and travelers from across the world witness this festival through Ganges with a boat ride.
